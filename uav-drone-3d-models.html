FAQ

What are UAV 3D models used for?

Which file formats should I check before buying?

Open the TurboSquid product page and verify 3ds Max, Blender, Maya, Cinema 4D, FBX, OBJ, Unreal Engine, Unity, textures, polygon count and rigging notes.

How can I verify rigging or moving parts before buying?

Check the TurboSquid preview renders, product description, file list and object hierarchy notes to confirm moving parts such as propellers, wheels, doors, landing gear or control surfaces.
