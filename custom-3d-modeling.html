How to request a quote

  • Send reference images, drawings or links.
  • Specify target software and formats: 3ds Max, Blender, Maya, Cinema 4D, FBX, OBJ, Unreal or Unity.
  • Explain the required level of detail and camera distance.
  • Mention whether rigging, animation or separated moving parts are needed.

What affects the price

Complexity, accuracy requirements, texture resolution, rigging, animation, number of variations and delivery format affect the quote.
