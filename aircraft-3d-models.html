Aircraft 3D Models for 3ds Max, Blender, Maya, Cinema 4D, FBX and OBJ

Aircraft 3D models serve a wide range of production needs: aviation visualization, flight animation, aerospace explainer videos, airport scenes, defense simulation, VFX shots and real-time training environments. Buyers often compare aircraft by format, rigging, interior detail, landing gear, engine detail, texture quality and scale. The 3D Molier catalog includes civilian aircraft, military transport aircraft, fighter jets, helicopters, bombers, business jets, airport collections, aircraft shelters and engine assets that support technical and cinematic workflows.

Aircraft models for technical visualization

Aircraft assets can communicate engineering concepts, aircraft layout, airport operations or flight scenarios. Detailed surfaces and clean materials help when cameras move close to wings, cockpit areas, landing gear or engines.

Rigged aircraft and helicopter models

Rigging is useful for landing gear, rotor movement, flight poses, doors and propellers. When animation is part of the project, check whether the product is rigged on TurboSquid.

Aircraft for Unreal Engine, Unity and simulation

For real-time scenes, prioritize manageable polygon counts, correct scale and exchange formats such as FBX or OBJ. High-detail models can be optimized for engine use when needed.

File formats to check

Look for 3ds Max, Blender, Maya, Cinema 4D, FBX, OBJ, textures, materials, UV mapping and PBR support.
